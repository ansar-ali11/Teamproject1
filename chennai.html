About Chennai

Chennai, the capital of Tamil Nadu, is known for its deep-rooted traditions, stunning temples, and delicious cuisine. This bustling metropolis blends ancient and modern seamlessly, with landmarks like the historic Fort St. George and the vibrant Marina Beach. Chennai is also famous for its classical music and dance, hosting numerous festivals throughout the year that celebrate its rich culture. Explore its unique neighborhoods, savor local delicacies like dosa and filter coffee, and immerse yourself in the warmth of its people.

Must-Visit Places in Chennai

Fort St. George

Built in 1644, this historic fort is the first English fortress in India. Explore the museum that showcases the cityâ€™s colonial past and admire the architecture that stands as a testament to Chennai's rich history.

Marina Beach

One of the longest urban beaches in the world, Marina Beach is perfect for evening strolls and enjoying local snacks. Witness breathtaking sunrises and sunsets while immersing yourself in the local culture and traditions.

Kapaleeshwarar Temple

A stunning example of Dravidian architecture, this ancient temple dedicated to Lord Shiva is adorned with intricate sculptures and vibrant carvings, making it a must-visit for its spiritual ambiance and artistic beauty.

Government Museum

One of the oldest museums in India, it houses a rich collection of art, archaeology, and numismatics. The museum offers insights into the region's history and showcases artifacts that reflect the cultural heritage of Tamil Nadu.

Guindy National Park

A unique urban national park, it is home to diverse flora and fauna. Enjoy nature trails, spot animals like deer and blackbuck, and appreciate the serene environment within the bustling city.

Sriperumbudur

The birthplace of Sri Ramanuja, this town features the beautiful Rajagopalaswamy temple and is an important pilgrimage site. Its tranquil environment and spiritual significance attract visitors seeking peace and devotion.
