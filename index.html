Popular Destinations

Mumbai

Experience the vibrant energy of Mumbai, India's bustling metropolis known as the "City of Dreams." Explore iconic landmarks like the Gateway of India, enjoy the vibrant culture at Marine Drive, and delve into the rich history at Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj Terminus. Don't miss out on the delicious street food and the lively local markets!

Hyderabad

Explore Hyderabad, the City of Pearls, where history meets modernity. Visit the majestic Charminar, delve into the opulence of the Chowmahalla Palace, and enjoy the tranquility of the Hussain Sagar Lake. Don't forget to taste the famous Hyderabadi biryani and shop for exquisite pearls and bangles in the old city bazaars.

Agra

Discover Agra, home to one of the world’s most iconic monuments, the Taj Mahal. This city is rich in history and culture, offering a glimpse into India’s Mughal heritage. Explore the magnificent Agra Fort, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, and stroll through the beautiful gardens of Mehtab Bagh for stunning views of the Taj.

Featured Hotels

Taj Hotel

The Taj Mahal Palace Hotel, an architectural marvel, stands majestically by the Gateway of India. With its iconic domes and luxurious interiors, this historic hotel has hosted royalty, celebrities, and dignitaries from around the world. Known for its impeccable service and grandeur, the Taj Hotel offers a blend of rich heritage and modern luxury, making it a symbol of Mumbai's elegance and charm.

six senses fort barwara hotel

The Six Senses Fort Barwara, nestled in the heart of Rajasthan, is a 14th-century fort transformed into an all-suite luxury resort. Guests can enjoy breathtaking views of the lake, the Chauth ka Barwara temple, and the Aravali hills from their suites. The resort features a Six Senses Spa, a fitness center, an aerial yoga pavilion, and an outdoor swimming pool. the Six Senses Fort Barwara offers a serene escape focused on wellbeing and relaxation.

kaldan samudra palace

The Kaldan Samudra Palace, located in Mahabalipuram, is a luxurious 5-star resort set on 10 acres of land by the sea. This palatial property combines Rajasthani architecture with modern amenities, offering a blend of traditional elegance and contemporary comfort. Guests can enjoy stunning sea views, an outdoor swimming pool, a Six Senses Spa, and a variety of dining options. personalized services, ensuring an unforgettable experience.
